# Math Foundations Quiz

## Order of Operations
What is the value of 2 + 3 * 4?

- 20
- 14
- 18
- 24

Answer: 2
Type: single
Time: 15
Explanation: Multiplication happens before addition.

## Prime Numbers
Which numbers are prime?

- 2
- 9
- 11
- 15

Answer: 1,3
Type: multiple
Time: 25
Explanation: 2 and 11 are prime numbers.

## Fraction Simplification
What is 8/12 simplified?

- 4/6
- 2/3
- 3/4
- 1/2

Answer: 2
Type: single
Time: 20
Explanation: Divide numerator and denominator by 4.

## Even Numbers
Which of the following are even numbers?

- 7
- 10
- 13
- 24

Answer: 2,4
Type: multiple
Time: 20
Explanation: 10 and 24 are even.

## Percentage
What is 25% of 200?

- 25
- 40
- 50
- 75

Answer: 3
Type: single
Time: 20
Explanation: 25% of 200 is 50.

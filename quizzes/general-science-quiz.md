# General Science Quiz

## States of Matter
Which of these are states of matter?

- Solid
- Liquid
- Energy
- Gas

Answer: 1,2,4
Type: multiple
Time: 25
Explanation: Solid, liquid, and gas are classic states of matter.

## Planet Closest to the Sun
Which planet is closest to the Sun?

- Venus
- Mercury
- Earth
- Mars

Answer: 2
Type: single
Time: 15
Explanation: Mercury is closest to the Sun.

## Human Body System
The heart is part of which system?

- Respiratory
- Circulatory
- Digestive
- Nervous

Answer: 2
Type: single
Time: 20
Explanation: The heart pumps blood through the circulatory system.

## Scientific Units
Which are SI base units?

- meter
- second
- newton
- kelvin

Answer: 1,2,4
Type: multiple
Time: 30
Explanation: meter, second, and kelvin are SI base units; newton is derived.

## Water Boiling Point
At standard atmospheric pressure, water boils at:

- 80 C
- 90 C
- 100 C
- 120 C

Answer: 3
Type: single
Time: 15
Explanation: Water boils at 100 C at standard pressure.

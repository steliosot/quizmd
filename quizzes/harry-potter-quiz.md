# 🦉 Harry Potter Quiz

## Question 1
What is the name of Harry Potter’s owl?

- Crookshanks
- Hedwig
- Scabbers
- Errol

Answer: 2
Type: single
Time: 30
Explanation: Hedwig is Harry Potter’s snowy owl. Hagrid gives her to Harry when he first enters the wizarding world.

## Question 2
What house is Harry Potter sorted into?

- Slytherin
- Ravenclaw
- Gryffindor
- Hufflepuff

Answer: 3
Type: single
Time: 30
Explanation: Harry is sorted into Gryffindor, the house known for bravery and courage, despite the Sorting Hat considering Slytherin.

## Question 3
Who is the headmaster of Hogwarts at the start of the series?

- Severus Snape
- Albus Dumbledore
- Sirius Black
- Lucius Malfoy

Answer: 2
Type: single
Time: 30
Explanation: Albus Dumbledore is the headmaster of Hogwarts and one of the most powerful and respected wizards in the series.

## Question 4

- Platform 8
- Platform 9

- Seeker

## Question 6
Which of the following are Unforgivable Curses?

- Expelliarmus
- Crucio
- Avada Kedavra
- Imperio

Answer: 2,3,4
Type: multiple
Time: 40
Explanation: The three Unforgivable Curses are Crucio (torture), Avada Kedavra (killing), and Imperio (control). Expelliarmus is a harmless disarming spell.

## Question 7
Who kills Dumbledore?

- Draco Malfoy
- Bellatrix Lestrange
- Severus Snape
- Voldemort

Answer: 3
Type: single
Time: 40
Explanation: Severus Snape kills Dumbledore as part of a planned agreement to protect Draco and maintain his cover.

## Question 8
Which of the following are Horcruxes?

- Tom Riddle’s Diary
- Nagini
- The Sword of Gryffindor
- Harry Potter

Answer: 1,2,4
Type: multiple
Time: 40
Explanation: Horcruxes are objects containing pieces of Voldemort’s soul. Harry unknowingly becomes one, while the sword is not a Horcrux.

## Question 9
Which spell is used to disarm an opponent?

- Lumos
- Expelliarmus
- Accio
- Stupefy

Answer: 2
Type: single
Time: 30
Explanation: Expelliarmus is the disarming spell that forces an opponent to release whatever they are holding.

## Question 10
Which of the following were members of the Marauders?

- James Potter
- Sirius Black
- Remus Lupin
- Severus Snape

Answer: 1,2,3
Type: multiple
Time: 40
Explanation: The Marauders were James Potter, Sirius Black, Remus Lupin, and Peter Pettigrew. Severus Snape was not a member.
